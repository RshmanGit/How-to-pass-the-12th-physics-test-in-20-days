### Q1. KDIGO / AKIN staging of AKI and CKD (5m)
<!-- id: sys04-crit-q1 -->

**Restated:** Outline the KDIGO (AKIN) staging of acute kidney injury and the KDIGO staging of chronic
kidney disease.

- **Principle (1m):** **KDIGO** harmonised the earlier **RIFLE/AKIN** systems. **AKI** is staged on the
  **worse** of two axes — the rise in **serum creatinine** and the fall in **urine output**. **CKD** is
  staged on **GFR (G)** *and* **albuminuria (A)**, because both independently predict outcome. (1m)

- **AKI staging — KDIGO/AKIN (2m):**

| Stage | Serum creatinine | Urine output |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | 1.5–1.9× baseline, **or** ↑ ≥ 0.3 mg/dL | < 0.5 mL/kg/h for 6–12 h |
| **2** | 2.0–2.9× baseline | < 0.5 mL/kg/h for ≥ 12 h |
| **3** | ≥ 3.0× baseline, **or** creatinine ≥ 4.0 mg/dL, **or** initiation of **RRT** | < 0.3 mL/kg/h for ≥ 24 h, **or** anuria ≥ 12 h |

  (2m for the full table; 1m partial if stages named without the creatinine/urine thresholds.)

- **CKD staging — GFR categories (1m):**

| GFR category | eGFR (mL/min/1.73 m²) | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **G1** | ≥ 90 | normal/high (with kidney damage) |
| **G2** | 60–89 | mildly decreased |
| **G3a / G3b** | 45–59 / 30–44 | mild–moderate / moderate–severe |
| **G4** | 15–29 | severely decreased |
| **G5** | < 15 | kidney failure (ESRD) |

  (1m for the G1–G5 table.)

- **CKD albuminuria + use (1m):** also staged by **ACR — A1** (< 30 mg/g, normal), **A2** (30–300,
  moderate), **A3** (> 300, severe). Staging guides **monitoring, drug dosing, referral, and RRT
  planning**; the combined **GFR × albuminuria grid** stratifies prognostic risk. (1m)
