## Important / repeated questions (high board frequency)

- **Faraday's two laws + Lenz's law statement** (Q7, Q8) — Lenz's law linked to energy
  conservation is a perennial 2-3m.
- **Motional EMF ε = Blv** — both flux method and Lorentz-force method (Q13). ~80% of papers.
- **Rod rotating about one end ε = ½Bωl²** (Q14) — derivation + numerical (Q20, the 400 rad/s,
  100 V NCERT problem).
- **Self-inductance of a solenoid L = μ₀n²Al** (Q15) — repeats every 2-3 years.
- **Mutual inductance of coaxial solenoids M = μ₀N₁N₂A/l** with reciprocity (Q16).
- **Energy stored U = ½LI² and density B²/2μ₀** (Q17).
- **AC generator principle + ε = NBAω sin ωt** (Q18) — recurring 5m.
- **Eddy currents: definition, lamination, applications** (Q10) and the case study (Q26).
