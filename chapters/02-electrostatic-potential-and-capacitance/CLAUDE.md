### Key derivations to memorise
- V = kq/r (point charge); V = (1/4πε₀)(p cosθ/r²) (dipole).
- C = ε₀A/d → Kε₀A/d (parallel plate).
- 1/C_eq = Σ1/Cᵢ (series); C_eq = ΣCᵢ (parallel).
- U = Q²/2C = ½CV²; u = ½ε₀E².
- U_dipole = −pE cosθ.
