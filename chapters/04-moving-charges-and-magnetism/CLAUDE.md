# Chapter 4 — Moving Charges and Magnetism (Content Map)

> Class 12 CBSE Physics pass-kit. This file maps the question bank for Chapter 4 and flags the high-frequency board questions. Pointers only — the answers live in `question-bank/`.

## Chapter at a glance

- **Chapter:** 4 — Moving Charges and Magnetism
- **Unit:** III — Magnetic Effects of Current and Magnetism (Ch 4 + 5), **~17 marks** for the unit; Ch 4 typically contributes **~6 marks**.
- **Difficulty:** Medium. Formula-heavy but the derivations and numericals are highly predictable and repeat year to year.
- **Syllabus note (2025-26):** Cyclotron (4.4.2), Velocity selector (4.4.1), Toroid (4.8.2), and Magnetic dipole moment of a revolving electron (4.10.3) are **DELETED** and are intentionally **excluded** here. (The upstream topic list mentioned the cyclotron; it is not in the current syllabus, so it was dropped.)

## Current in-syllabus topics covered

- Biot–Savart law; field on the axis of a circular current loop
- Ampere's circuital law → long straight wire; solenoid (qualitative)
- Lorentz force; circular/helical motion of a charge in `B`
- Force between two parallel currents → definition of the ampere
- Force on a current-carrying conductor
- Torque on a current loop; magnetic moment
- Moving-coil galvanometer; conversion to ammeter/voltmeter; sensitivity
