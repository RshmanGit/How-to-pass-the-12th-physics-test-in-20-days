## Important / repeated questions (the pass-kit core)

- **Distinguish dia/para/ferromagnetism** (susceptibility sign+size, field behaviour, T-dependence, examples) — appears almost every year (Q7).
- **Curie's law** χ ∝ 1/T — statement + graph + numerical M₂/M₁ = (B₂/B₁)(T₁/T₂) (Q8, Q23).
- **Permanent magnet vs electromagnet** material (retentivity/coercivity, hysteresis loop shape); soft-iron core (Q9, Q10).
- **Earth's magnetism** — define declination/dip/Bₕ; relations B=Bₕ/cos δ, tan δ = Bᵥ/Bₕ; dip at pole/equator (Q11, Q12, Q21, Q22, Q26).
- **Solenoid magnetic moment** m = NIA numerical (800 turns, 2.5e-4 m², 3.0 A -> 0.6 J/T) (Q18).
- **Torque numericals** τ = mB sin θ (find m or τ) (Q19, Q20).
- **μᵣ = 1 + χ** classification numericals (Q2, Q25).

## Key derivations (mark-carrying)

- **Bar magnet as equivalent solenoid** -> axial field B = (μ₀/4π)(2m/r³), m = NIA (Q14, 5m flagship derivation).
- **Torque on a dipole** τ = m × B (Q15).
- **Potential energy** U = −m·B = −mB cos θ; stable/unstable equilibria (Q16).
- **Time period of oscillating magnet** T = 2π√(I/mB) (Q17).
- **Earth's field component relations** (Q12).
