## Key derivations / must-know results
- `Id = e0 (d phiE/dt)`; for a capacitor `Id = Ic = dq/dt` (Q16).
- Modified Ampere-Maxwell law: `closed integral B.dl = mu0(Ic + e0 d phiE/dt)`.
- `c = 1/sqrt(mu0 e0) = 3 x 10^8 m/s`; in medium `v = c/n` (Q17).
- `E = cB`, `E0/B0 = c`; energy split `u_E = u_B`; `u_avg = (1/2)e0 E0^2 = B0^2/(2 mu0)` (Q14).
- Radiation pressure: absorbing `I/c`, reflecting `2I/c` (Q15).
