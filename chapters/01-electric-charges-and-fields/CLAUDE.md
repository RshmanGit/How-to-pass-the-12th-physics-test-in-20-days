## Important / repeated questions (the pass-kit core)

**Must-know derivations (appear almost every year):**
- Gauss's law → field of a uniformly charged spherical shell (outside / surface / inside) + E-vs-r graph — `03-derivations.md`
- Gauss's law → field of an infinite line charge, E = λ/2πε₀r — `03-derivations.md`
- Gauss's law → field of an infinite plane sheet, E = σ/2ε₀ — `03-derivations.md`
- Electric field on axial line of a dipole, E = 2kp/r³ — `03-derivations.md`
- Electric field on equatorial line of a dipole, E = kp/r³ — `03-derivations.md`
- Torque on a dipole, τ = pE sinθ (= p×E) — `03-derivations.md`
- Potential energy of a dipole U = −pE cosθ; work to flip = 2pE — `03-derivations.md`

## Key derivations checklist (mark-carrying steps)

- Spherical shell: state Gauss → concentric Gaussian sphere → 3 cases → boxed E_out, E=0 inside, σ/ε₀ at surface → graph.
- Line charge: coaxial cylinder → only curved face contributes → E = λ/2πε₀r.
- Plane sheet: pillbox → 2EA = σA/ε₀ → E = σ/2ε₀ (note σ/ε₀ for a conductor).
- Dipole axial/equatorial: superpose two point-charge fields → short-dipole limit → boxed result; remember axial = 2 × equatorial.
