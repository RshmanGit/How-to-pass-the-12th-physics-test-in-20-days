## Important / repeated questions (high board frequency)

- **Define work function / threshold frequency / stopping potential** (Q7) — perennial 1-3m.
- **Einstein's photoelectric equation hν = φ₀ + KE_max** (Q12) — most-asked derivation; must
  explain how it accounts for threshold, instantaneity, intensity-independence of KE_max.
- **Stopping potential vs frequency graph → read h, ν₀, φ₀** (Q13) — recurring graph question;
  slope = h/e, parallel lines for two metals.
- **Photocurrent vs voltage and vs intensity graphs** (Q14) — common 2-3m; common stopping
  potential, saturation ∝ intensity.
- **de Broglie wavelength λ = h/√(2mqV) derivation** (Q15) — repeats every 2-3 years.
- **Davisson–Germer experiment** (Q16) — qualitative description + 54 V / 50° / 0.165 nm.
- **Wave theory fails to explain photoelectric effect** (Q9) and **frequency vs intensity
  effects** (Q1, Q11) — frequent short-answer/MCQ.
