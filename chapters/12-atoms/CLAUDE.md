## Important / repeated questions (the pass-kit core)

- **State Bohr's postulates** (3 postulates: stationary orbits, `L = nh/2π`, frequency condition)
  — near-certain (Q9). Foundation for every derivation below.
- **Derive energy `E_n = -13.6/n² eV`** + significance of negative sign — flagship 5m (Q19).
- **Derive radius `r_n ∝ n²`** from Coulomb = centripetal + quantisation (Q18, 3m).
- **Rydberg relation & spectral series** — `1/λ = R(1/n1² − 1/n2²)`; name Lyman/Balmer/Paschen and
  their regions UV/visible/IR (Q13, Q21, Q23).
- **Rutherford alpha-scattering** — apparatus, observations, conclusions, hyperbolic trajectory
  (Q22, 5m with diagram).
- **Distance of closest approach** — definition + `r0 = (1/4πε0)(2Ze²/K)`; effect of doubling K
  (Q15, Q16, Q25, Q26).
- **de Broglie justification of quantisation** — `2πr = nλ ⇒ L = nh/2π` (Q20, Q31).
- **Repeated numericals:** closest approach (5.5 MeV/Z=79 → 4.0×10⁻¹⁴ m; 8 MeV/Z=80 → 2.9×10⁻¹⁴ m);
  Balmer shortest 365 nm & H-alpha 656 nm; Lyman-α 121.5 nm; 1→4 excitation 12.75 eV / 97 nm;
  ground-state KE +13.6 / PE −27.2 eV; max lines `n(n-1)/2` (Q24–Q32).
- **Common short theory:** why classical Rutherford atom is unstable (Q10); two limitations of
  Bohr's model (Q11).

## Key derivations (mark-carrying)

- **Radius of nth orbit** `r_n = (ε0 h²/πme²)n² = 0.53 n² Å`, show `r ∝ n²` (Q18).
- **Total energy** `E = KE + PE = -e²/(8πε0 r) ⇒ E_n = -13.6/n² eV`; KE = −E, PE = 2E (Q19).
- **Bohr quantisation from de Broglie** `2πr = nλ ⇒ mvr = nh/2π` (Q20).
- **Rydberg formula** from `hc/λ = E_{n2} − E_{n1}` (Q21).
- **Speed `v ∝ 1/n`, frequency of revolution `f ∝ 1/n³`** (Q4, Q5, Q30).
